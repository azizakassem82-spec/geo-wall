WQI Methodology & AI Logic

1. Mathematical Formula

The Water Quality Index (WQI) is computed using the weighted arithmetic index method:

WQI = Σ (wi * qi) / Σ wi
  • wi: Unit weight of the i-th parameter.
  • qi: Quality rating of the i-th parameter.
